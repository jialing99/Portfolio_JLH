Animation


Matrix


In this short animation, I wanted to recreate the mythic movie called Matrix. Specifically, the scene when Neo, the protagonist, needs to make the decision between the two pills, blue and red, that represent different ways of reality. The blue pill represents the reality that Neo know. In that part, I use the effect called CC Kaleida to give a kaleidoscope effect in the background. Also, the circle is moving in a circle and its colors change when the song starts to distort. The song is called "Happy Together" by Turtles. I picked this song because in the beginning, it starts with a sad and melancholic melody but the lyrics represent something happy. All of these represent that the reality of Neo, which is repetitive and boring but is ok. However, he needs something more. Consequently, he pick the red pill, which is intense ("Le Castle Vania - John Wick Medley" song) and chaotic (letters that represent the matrix, reality that Morpheus know).
